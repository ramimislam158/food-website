Our Story

Born in Banani,
obsessed with
flavour.

YOSHI is not just a restaurant — it is a statement about what food in Dhaka can be.

YOSHI was born from a simple but radical idea: what if Dhaka had a pan-Asian restaurant that refused to compromise? Not just "inspired by" Asian flavours — but truly, obsessively faithful to them.

We opened our doors in Banani in 2025 with one mission: to bring the soul of Tokyo's ramen alleys, Seoul's bulgogi grills, and Bangkok's wonton shops under one roof — and to do it flawlessly, every single time.

Every broth is simmered for hours. Every marinade is crafted with care. Every plate is finished with intention. No shortcuts. No compromises. Just food that makes you close your eyes on the first bite.

And we do it fast — our average kitchen-to-table time is under 10 minutes. Because great food should never make you wait.
